# Sales Revenue Dashboard - Power BI

## Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides insights into sales performance across different regions, countries, and customers. The dashboard helps analyze revenue trends, customer contributions, and regional performance to support business decision-making.

## Features

### 1. Monthly Revenue Analysis

* Tracks revenue trends over time.
* Identifies high and low performing months.
* Helps understand sales growth patterns.

### 2. Revenue & Quantity by Country

* Compares revenue generated from different countries.
* Analyzes product quantity sold across countries.
* Highlights top-performing markets.

### 3. Revenue by Customers

* Identifies customers contributing the highest revenue.
* Helps understand customer value and purchasing behavior.
* Supports customer segmentation analysis.

### 4. Revenue by Region

* Visualizes revenue distribution across regions.
* Compares regional performance.
* Helps identify growth opportunities.

## Tools & Technologies

* Power BI Desktop
* Data Visualization
* Data Analysis
* Business Intelligence

## Key Insights

* Revenue trends can be monitored monthly.
* Top-performing countries and regions can be identified.
* Customer-wise revenue contribution can be analyzed.
* Supports data-driven business decisions.
